Federico Reyes (frey_s) is a Venezuelan pianist, producer & composer based in London. Through modern experimental approaches, he exhibits a musical report about a trip that started when he was born. He started playing piano and composing around the age of 12. Today, he incorporates original synths recordings with obscure manipulated samples. His influences from jazz, latin music, ambient and hip hop, merge to produce a mellow, nostalgic, dreamy sound.

He studied Music, Sound and Technology at City University of London. In London, he worked as a luxury hotel pianist, music teacher, and producer for various independent projects. He is also a founding member of the Barcelona-based record label Lacreu Records.
